# Study Material Framework

This skill is invoked for deep-dive, highly technical topics derived from primary study notes.

## Tone & Voice
- Extremely technical, authoritative, and analytical.
- Assume the audience consists of Senior Software Engineers (SDE-3 level).
- No fluff, no generic hype words. Focus on architecture, trade-offs, and system design.

## Structure
- Hook: A bold, counter-intuitive technical statement or an architectural problem.
- Body: A dense, bulleted breakdown of the technical mechanism, trade-offs, and scaling implications.
- Conclusion: A specific question asking peers how they handle this pattern or edge case in production.
