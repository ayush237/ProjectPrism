# Virality Framework

This skill is invoked for trends, hot topics, and highly engaging hooks derived from Database C.

## Tone & Voice
- Fast-paced, punchy, and highly opinionated.
- Leverages analogies heavily to make complex concepts immediately relatable.
- Designed for maximum retention and rapid scrolling environments.

## Structure
- Hook: Must capture attention within the first 3 seconds (or first 2 lines). High-energy, contrarian, or surprising.
- Body: Rapid pacing. Use short sentences, strong visual cues (if video), or single-line formatting (if text). Emphasize "the catch" or the core problem mid-way to retain attention.
- Conclusion: A strong, definitive takeaway or a polarizing question designed to maximize comment engagement.
