# Series Framework

This skill is invoked for multi-part, sequential topics derived from Database B.

## Tone & Voice
- Educational and narrative-driven. 
- Builds upon previous context. 
- Accessible but retaining technical accuracy.

## Structure
- Hook: Reference the ongoing series to build continuity (e.g., "In part 3 of our Distributed Systems series...").
- Body: Introduce the new concept, link it back to the previous part, and provide a clear example.
- Conclusion: Tease the next part of the series and give the audience a small homework task or thought experiment.
