# Instagram Algorithm & Style Rules

To ensure maximum engagement and retention on Instagram Reels, you must strictly follow these structural and pacing constraints for your scripts:

## 1. The 3-Second Hook
The script must hook the viewer immediately in the first 3 seconds. 
*   **Visual Cue:** The visual cue must be dynamic (e.g., text pop-up with a bold claim, fast zoom, sudden visual shift).
*   **Audio/Script:** Start with a high-energy or counter-intuitive hook.

## 2. Pacing & Text
*   **Rapid Transitions:** The "Visual/Audio Cue" column must include transitions or visual changes every 3-4 seconds to keep the viewer visually stimulated.
*   **On-Screen Text:** Any text that pops up on the screen must be **5 words maximum** at a time. Do not crowd the screen with text.

## 3. The Retention Mechanic
*   **Mid-Video Problem:** In the middle of the script, you must introduce a "wait, here is the problem" or "but there's a catch" moment. This re-engages the viewer's attention before delivering the final solution or core value proposition.
